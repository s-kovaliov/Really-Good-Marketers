How does Really Good Marketers tracking work?

Our tracking is an industry’s first. In order to safeguard our longevity and protect our users from the incoming ad-pocalypse 2.0, we developed the Really Good Marketers tracking without the need for any cookies. This is done by directly integrating with your GoHighLevel account and using your leads’ metadata to attribute them to individual ads. In short - you’ll be in a good spot - indefinitely.

How is data tracking different from native tracking on other platforms (such as Facebook Ads Manager)?

You probably noticed sometime in 2021 that ad tracking just wasn’t what it was compared to the early days of Facebook. This is because Apple and other hardware companies limited the tracking ability of cookies*. Fast forward to today and ad platforms are attempting to minimize this impact by implementing what’s called server side tracking. This is not only difficult to set up but it’s just not scalable. With every increase in page traffic, not only will you pay more, but the risk of un-tracked leads increases.

Really Good Marketers avoids this by avoiding cookies and server side tracking all together. Instead we use GoHighLevel to track leads, thereby creating a custom solution specific to GHL users.

*Cookies are bits of code that collect information. In the case of Facebook ads, Facebook cookies collected information about your website visitor (where they came from, which ads they clicked, ages, gender, etc.).
